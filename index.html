      рҹ“җ How to Calculate Oxidation States

      рҹ“Ӣ Rules

      1. Sum of oxidation states in a neutral compound = 0
      2. Sum of oxidation states in an ion = charge on the ion
      3. Oxygen (O) = -2 (except in peroxides where it's -1)
      4. Hydrogen (H) = +1 (except in metal hydrides where it's -1)
      5. Group 1 metals = +1, Group 2 metals = +2

      рҹ§Ә Worked Example: MnOвӮ„вҒ»

      Find oxidation state of Mn in MnOвӮ„вҒ»

      Let Mn = x
      4 Oxygen atoms: 4 Г— (-2) = -8
      Total charge on ion = -1

      Equation: x + (-8) = -1
      x - 8 = -1
      x = -1 + 8
      x = +7

      вң… Manganese in MnOвӮ„вҒ» = +7 (hence Manganate(VII))

      рҹ§Ә Worked Example: CrвӮӮOвӮҮВІвҒ»

      Find oxidation state of Cr in CrвӮӮOвӮҮВІвҒ»

      Let each Cr = x (2 Cr atoms = 2x)
      7 Oxygen atoms: 7 Г— (-2) = -14
      Total charge = -2

      Equation: 2x + (-14) = -2
      2x - 14 = -2
      2x = 12
      x = +6

      вң… Each Chromium = +6 (hence Dichromate(VI))
