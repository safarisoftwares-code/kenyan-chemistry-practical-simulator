      💧 Ions Guide — Cations, Anions & Complex Ions

      🟢 Cations

      Na⁺ || Sodium — colourless aq
      K⁺ || Potassium — colourless aq
      NH₄⁺ || Ammonium — colourless aq
      Ca²⁺ || Calcium — colourless aq
      Mg²⁺ || Magnesium — colourless aq
      Al³⁺ || Aluminium — colourless aq
      Zn²⁺ || Zinc — colourless aq
      Fe²⁺ || Iron(II) — pale green aq
      Fe³⁺ || Iron(III) — yellow-brown aq
      Pb²⁺ || Lead(II) — colourless aq
      Cu²⁺ || Copper(II) — blue aq
      Ba²⁺ || Barium — colourless aq

      🟠 Anions

      OH⁻ || Hydroxide — colourless aq
      SO₄²⁻ || Sulphate — colourless aq
      SO₃²⁻ || Sulphite — colourless aq
      CO₃²⁻ || Carbonate — colourless aq
      Cl⁻ || Chloride — colourless aq
      Br⁻ || Bromide — colourless aq
      I⁻ || Iodide — colourless dilute aq
      NO₃⁻ || Nitrate — colourless aq
      HCO₃⁻ || Hydrogen carbonate — colourless aq

      🔄 Complex Ions

      [Al(OH)₄]⁻ || Al(OH)₃ + OH⁻ → colourless
      [Zn(OH)₄]²⁻ || Zn(OH)₂ + 2OH⁻ → colourless
      [Pb(OH)₄]²⁻ || Pb(OH)₂ + 2OH⁻ → colourless
      [Cu(NH₃)₄]²⁺ || Cu(OH)₂ + 4NH₃ → deep blue
      [Ag(NH₃)₂]⁺ || AgCl + 2NH₃ → colourless

      🔬 Precipitates Guide

      🟡 Carbonates

      Ca²⁺ || CaCO₃ white
      Mg²⁺ || MgCO₃ white
      Zn²⁺ || ZnCO₃ white
      Fe²⁺ || FeCO₃ green-grey
      Cu²⁺ || CuCO₃ blue-green
      Pb²⁺ || PbCO₃ white
      Ba²⁺ || BaCO₃ white

      🟠 Sulphates

      Ca²⁺ || CaSO₄ white
      Ba²⁺ || BaSO₄ white
      Pb²⁺ || PbSO₄ white

      ⚪ Chlorides

      Pb²⁺ || PbCl₂ white
      Ag⁺ || AgCl white

      🔵 Hydroxides

      Al³⁺ || Al(OH)₃ white gel (→[Al(OH)₄]⁻)
      Zn²⁺ || Zn(OH)₂ white (→[Zn(OH)₄]²⁻)
      Fe²⁺ || Fe(OH)₂ dirty green
      Fe³⁺ || Fe(OH)₃ red-brown
      Cu²⁺ || Cu(OH)₂ blue (→[Cu(NH₃)₄]²⁺)
      Pb²⁺ || Pb(OH)₂ white (→[Pb(OH)₄]²⁻)

      🔄 Complex & Excess

      Al(OH)₃ + OH⁻ → [Al(OH)₄]⁻
      Zn(OH)₂ + 2OH⁻ → [Zn(OH)₄]²⁻
      Pb(OH)₂ + 2OH⁻ → [Pb(OH)₄]²⁻
      Cu(OH)₂ + 4NH₃ → [Cu(NH₃)₄]²⁺
      AgCl + 2NH₃ → [Ag(NH₃)₂]⁺
