1. Introduction

Safari Softwares ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chemistry Practical Simulator handles information.

2. No Data Collection

Our Chemistry Practical Simulator operates entirely within your web browser. We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Location data
  • Browsing history
  • Device information
  • IP addresses

3. Local Storage

The simulator uses your browser's localStorage only for:

  • Premium activation status (stored locally on your device)
  • Your activation key (to verify premium access)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Premium Activation

When you activate a premium key, your device generates a unique fingerprint used only for device verification. This fingerprint is stored locally and is not transmitted to any server.

5. No Cookies

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking services.

6. No Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate with any external services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

7. Children's Privacy

This educational software is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults.
