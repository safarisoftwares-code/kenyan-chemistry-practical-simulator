3.2 How localStorage Data Is Used

  • All localStorage data is used exclusively for the Software's core functionality (Premium verification, device counting, admin key management);
  • This data is never transmitted to Safari Softwares or any third-party server;
  • This data is never shared between different websites or browser profiles;
  • You can view this data at any time using your browser's Developer Tools (F12 → Application → Local Storage);
  • You can delete this data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage (note: this will deactivate Premium access).

3.3 How to Clear localStorage

You can clear all localStorage data stored by the Software by:

  1. Opening your browser's Developer Tools (F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I);
  2. Navigating to the "Application" or "Storage" tab;
  3. Selecting "Local Storage" in the left sidebar;
  4. Selecting the domain where the Software is loaded;
  5. Clicking "Clear" or manually deleting individual items.

Alternatively, you can clear all browser data through your browser's Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data.

4. Premium Activation & Device Fingerprinting

4.1 Device Fingerprint

When you activate a Premium Activation Key, the Software generates a unique "device fingerprint" to identify your device for the purpose of enforcing the two-device limit per key. This fingerprint is created from:

  • Screen dimensions and color depth;
  • Your system's timezone setting;
  • The number of CPU cores (hardware concurrency);
  • Your operating system platform identifier;
  • Your browser's language setting.

These data points are combined and hashed into a string like "DEV-ABC123DEF456". The original data points are not stored — only the resulting hash is kept. This fingerprint:

  • Is stored only in your browser's localStorage;
  • Is never transmitted to any server;
  • Is used solely to count how many distinct devices have activated a given key;
  • Cannot be used to personally identify you or track you across websites.

4.2 Activation Key Processing

Premium activation is verified entirely within your browser. When you enter an Activation Key and password, the Software checks them against:

  • The keys.js file (a static list of valid keys distributed with the Software);
  • The safari_keys array in your browser's localStorage (for keys generated by the admin panel).

No network request is made to any server during activation. Your key and password are processed exclusively on your device.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. Under the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019) and similar laws, these rights may include:

  • Right of Access — The right to know what personal data is held about you;
  • Right to Rectification — The right to correct inaccurate personal data;
  • Right to Erasure — The right to request deletion of personal data ("right to be forgotten");
  • Right to Restrict Processing — The right to limit how your data is processed;
  • Right to Data Portability — The right to receive your data in a portable format;
  • Right to Object — The right to object to certain types of data processing.

However, since the Software does not collect personal data in the first place, most of these rights are inherently satisfied. Any localStorage data can be accessed, exported (via copy/paste from Developer Tools), or deleted by you at any time without needing to contact us.

For communications data (WhatsApp messages, emails), you may exercise your rights by contacting us at safarisoftwares@gmail.com. We will respond to verifiable requests within the timeframe required by applicable law.
