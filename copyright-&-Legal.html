3. Legal Framework

The Work is protected under the following legal instruments:

3.1 National Legislation (Kenya)

  • The Copyright Act (Cap 130), Laws of Kenya — Provides the primary framework for copyright protection in Kenya, including provisions for literary works (source code), artistic works (visual design), and audiovisual works (animations);
  • The Copyright (Amendment) Act, 2022 — Updates to Kenyan copyright law including digital rights management provisions;
  • The Kenya Industrial Property Act (Cap 509) — Provides additional protections for distinctive brand elements and trade dress;
  • The Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act, 2018 — Criminalizes unauthorized access to and interference with computer systems and data.

3.2 International Treaties & Conventions

  • The Berne Convention for the Protection of Literary and Artistic Works (1886, as amended) — To which Kenya is a signatory; provides automatic copyright protection across 181 member countries without requiring formal registration;
  • The Agreement on Trade-Related Aspects of Intellectual Property Rights (TRIPS Agreement, 1994) — Administered by the World Trade Organization (WTO); sets minimum standards for intellectual property protection;
  • The WIPO Copyright Treaty (WCT, 1996) — Addresses copyright in the digital environment, including protection for computer programs and databases;
  • The WIPO Performances and Phonograms Treaty (WPPT, 1996) — Provides additional protections relevant to digital works;
  • The African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) Protocol on Intellectual Property Rights — Provides IP protections across African Union member states.

3.3 Common Law Protections

  • Protection against passing off — Preventing others from misrepresenting their goods or services as being associated with Safari Softwares;
  • Protection of trade secrets — Safeguarding proprietary algorithms, databases, and business methods;
  • Moral rights — The right to be identified as the author of the Work and to object to derogatory treatment of the Work.
